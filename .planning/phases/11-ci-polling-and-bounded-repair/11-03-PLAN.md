Locked decisions:
- CI polling is explicit configured verification or configured sink evidence, not a background daemon.
- `source="ci"` entries must carry `ci://...` targets and must never be passed through shell command scanning/execution.
- A configured `ci_status` sink must generate concrete `source="ci"` verification command(s) that runtime plans can execute through the CI poll runner.
- Repair budgets default to zero unless explicitly configured.
