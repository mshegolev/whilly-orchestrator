Locked decisions:
- Repair attempts have explicit retry budgets and stop conditions.
- Repair remains disabled by default unless a budget is configured.
- A repair attempt creates a new repair task or escalates; it must not call `release_task()` or retry the same failed task.
- Exhausted budgets emit `repair.escalated`.
- Do not add new task statuses such as `REPAIRING`, `ESCALATED`, or `BLOCKED`.
- Plans 11-04 and 11-05 must wire `repair.attempt.completed` for terminal repair tasks; do not leave the event as a dead primitive.
</context>
